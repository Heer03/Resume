This project also gave me confidence in making some complex engineering drawings (drafting in CATIA V5).

As a part of the club, me and two other another members of the club are making the body of the robot. I feel that though small, this is a good step towards learing a new software. Aditionally, to adapt faster with these softwares and because of my curiosity to learn more about computer aided designing, I am currently learning generative shape design work bench in CATIA V5 and different tools in standard modelling in inventor.
